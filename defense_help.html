Self-Defense Tips

  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid distractions like headphones or phones.
  • Carry simple defense tools like pepper spray or a safety alarm.
  • Trust your instincts and stay away from situations that feel unsafe.
  • Learn how to escape grips by targeting vulnerable areas like the eyes or groin.
  • If attacked, focus on creating distance and finding help as quickly as possible.
